My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Raid 2

What I would describe as the best action movie ever put to film. Violent, gritty, fantastic camera work, captivating chartectors. An absolute 10/10 movie.

The Last Dragon

Growing up as a kid obsessed with ninjas like the TMNT, 3 Ninjas, and American Ninja, The Last Dragon took 80s cheese, fantasticially 80s music and martial arts and made what I will call one of the most rewatchable movies I have ever seen.
